About the Game

Therapists rely on exercises meant to be completed between sessions, but they are often difficult to engage with in practice. Cat Counselor takes these functionally inaccessible exercises and rebuilds them as interactive systems grounded in CBT and mindfulness.

Humble Origin Story

The Game

Each session revolves around your toolbox, where you listen to the client, read their state, and choose a tool that opens into a minigame based on a real therapy technique, with progress coming from understanding what the client is ready for rather than being told the correct answer.
